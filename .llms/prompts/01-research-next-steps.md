# Research next steps

## Purpose of this website
The goal of this website is to be my blog, where I will share my thoughts on Software Engineering, what I'm learning, patterns I use, thinks I often do, how I organise myself and etc.

On top of that, I also want to give an introduction about me, who I am, what I believe, and tell a little bit more about my story so that if someone gets curious about myself, they can read more about me and hopefully sympathise with me and create a better connection.

I also think this will help with others who might be evaluating me, to get a better perception of who I am, and if I have a good fit for a specific role, for example.
